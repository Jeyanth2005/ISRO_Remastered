ISRO Events

Engage with ISRO: Workshops, Seminars, and Public Events

Join us at our upcoming events to learn more about India's space program, interact with our scientists, and get a closer look at the technologies that are taking us to the stars.

November 22-24, 2025

National Space Science Symposium

ISRO is proud to host the National Space Science Symposium, bringing together scientists, engineers, and students from across the country to discuss the latest advancements in space science and technology. The event will be held at the U R Rao Satellite Centre, Bengaluru.

December 05, 2025

Public Viewing of G1 Mission Launch

Witness history in the making! A public viewing gallery will be open at the Satish Dhawan Space Centre for the launch of the first uncrewed Gaganyaan test flight (G1). Registration details will be announced shortly.

January 12-14, 2026

Indian Science Congress - Space Pavilion

Visit the ISRO pavilion at the annual Indian Science Congress. Explore interactive exhibits, scale models of our rockets and satellites, and participate in hands-on activities designed for all ages.
