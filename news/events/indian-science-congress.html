Explore the Universe at the ISRO Pavilion in the Indian Science Congress

Event Date: January 12-14, 2026

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) invites you to visit its state-of-the-art pavilion at the upcoming Indian Science Congress. This annual event is the largest gathering of the scientific community in the country, and the ISRO pavilion is always a star attraction.

Visitors to the pavilion will have the opportunity to embark on an interactive journey through India's space program. The exhibit will feature stunning scale models of our acclaimed launch vehicles like the PSLV and LVM3, and our historic spacecraft, including Chandrayaan-3, Mangalyaan, and the Gaganyaan crew module. The pavilion is designed to be an engaging experience for all ages, with hands-on activities, virtual reality simulations of space missions, and video presentations showcasing ISRO's latest achievements.

"The Indian Science Congress is the perfect venue for us to connect with the public and the scientific community. Our pavilion is not just a display of hardware; it's a celebration of India's scientific spirit and an invitation to the younger generation to join us in exploring the final frontier." - Director, Public Relations, ISRO

Throughout the three-day event, senior ISRO scientists and engineers will be present at the pavilion to interact with visitors and answer their questions. Don't miss this chance to get a closer look at the technology that is taking India to the Moon, Mars, and beyond.

About ISRO Events: ISRO is committed to public outreach and engagement. We regularly host events, workshops, and seminars to promote scientific temper and to keep the public informed about the latest developments in the Indian space program.
