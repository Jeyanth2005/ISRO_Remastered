ISRO to Host National Space Science Symposium in Bengaluru

Event Date: November 22-24, 2025

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is proud to announce that it will be hosting the prestigious National Space Science Symposium (NSSS) from November 22-24, 2025. The event will bring together the brightest minds in space science and technology from across India at the U R Rao Satellite Centre in Bengaluru.

The NSSS is a biennial event that provides a platform for scientists, engineers, researchers, and students to present their latest findings, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations. The symposium will feature plenary talks by eminent scientists, technical sessions on a wide range of topics including planetary science, astronomy, and earth sciences, as well as poster presentations.

"The National Space Science Symposium is a celebration of the scientific spirit that drives our space program. It is an essential forum for collaboration and for nurturing the next generation of space scientists who will lead India's future explorations." - Chairman, ISRO

A key highlight of the symposium will be a special session dedicated to the initial scientific results from the Aditya-L1 and Chandrayaan-3 missions. Registration for the event and the call for abstracts will open in mid-2025. Further details will be made available on the official ISRO website.

About ISRO Events: ISRO is committed to public outreach and engagement. We regularly host events, workshops, and seminars to promote scientific temper and to keep the public informed about the latest developments in the Indian space program.
