Mission Watch: India's Upcoming Journeys to Space

Stay informed about the next exciting chapters in India's space exploration story. All launch dates are tentative and subject to change based on mission readiness and weather conditions.

December 2025

Gaganyaan G1 (Uncrewed)

Launch Vehicle: LVM3
Mission: The first uncrewed developmental flight of the Gaganyaan spacecraft. This is a critical test of the human-rated launch vehicle, orbital module, and re-entry systems before the first crewed mission.

Early 2026

RISAT-2A

Launch Vehicle: PSLV
Mission: An advanced radar imaging satellite to enhance India's all-weather surveillance and Earth observation capabilities, crucial for disaster management and security.

Mid-2026

Gaganyaan G2 (Uncrewed with Vyommitra)

Launch Vehicle: LVM3
Mission: The second uncrewed test flight, featuring the humanoid robot 'Vyommitra' to simulate human functions and collect vital data on the crew module's environment.
