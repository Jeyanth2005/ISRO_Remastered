Frontier Focus: July 2025 Edition

Monthly Newsletter | Published August 01, 2025

Welcome to the July 2025 edition of Frontier Focus. This month, we highlight a major milestone in our propulsion technology and share insights from the Chandrayaan-3 science team.

Successful Test of New Semi-Cryogenic Engine

ISRO achieved a significant breakthrough with the successful long-duration test of its new semi-cryogenic engine. The engine, which uses refined kerosene (ISROSENE) as fuel and liquid oxygen as an oxidizer, was tested for over 800 seconds at our propulsion complex in Mahendragiri. This powerful and efficient engine is a critical building block for our Next Generation Launch Vehicle (NGLV), which will be capable of lifting much heavier payloads into orbit.

An Interview with the Chandrayaan-3 Science Team

In a special feature this month, we sat down with the lead scientists from the Chandrayaan-3 mission. They shared their experiences analyzing the data sent back by the Vikram lander and Pragyan rover. The team discussed the groundbreaking confirmation of Sulphur on the lunar surface and the ongoing analysis of the unique thermal profile of the lunar south pole, highlighting how these findings are reshaping our understanding of the Moon.
