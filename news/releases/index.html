Gaganyaan Mission: First Uncrewed Flight Test Scheduled

ISRO announces that the first uncrewed flight test of the Gaganyaan program, a crucial milestone in India's human spaceflight mission, is scheduled for the last quarter of this year.

Successful Launch of PSLV-C57 Carrying Aditya-L1 Satellite

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) successfully launched the PSLV-C57 vehicle carrying the Aditya-L1 satellite, India's first solar observatory, from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR, Sriharikota.
