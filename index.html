About ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the national space agency of India. It operates as the primary research and development arm of the Department of Space (DoS), which is directly overseen by the Prime Minister of India. ISRO is primarily responsible for space-based activities, space exploration, and the development of related technologies.

Our Missions

Chandrayaan

India's lunar exploration program, consisting of a series of missions to the Moon.

Mangalyaan

India's first interplanetary mission, the Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM).

GSLV

The Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle, used to launch satellites into geostationary orbit.
