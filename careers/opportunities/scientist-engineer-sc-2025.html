Recruitment for the post of Scientist/Engineer 'SC' - Computer Science

Advertisement No: ISRO:ICRB:01(EMC):2025
Location: Bengaluru
Application Deadline: May 19, 2025

Role Summary

As a Scientist/Engineer 'SC' in Computer Science at ISRO, you will be part of a dynamic team responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining the software systems that power India's space missions. This role offers a unique opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge projects of national importance, from satellite data processing to launch vehicle command and control systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement software for ground and onboard systems for satellites and launch vehicles.
  • Engage in the entire software development lifecycle, including requirement analysis, design, coding, testing, and documentation.
  • Work on real-time systems, database management, and network programming for mission-critical applications.
  • Conduct research and development in advanced computing fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Big Data analytics for space applications.
  • Ensure the reliability, security, and performance of all software systems through rigorous testing and quality assurance.

Essential Qualifications

  • B.E./B.Tech or equivalent qualification in Computer Science & Engineering with an aggregate minimum of 65% marks or CGPA 6.84/10.
  • Strong programming skills in languages like C++, Java, or Python.
  • Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and operating systems concepts.
  • Candidates must be Indian citizens.

Selection Process

The selection process will be based on a combination of factors. Initial screening will be conducted based on academic performance and other criteria provided in the online application. Shortlisted candidates will be called for a written test, and those who qualify will proceed to a final interview.
