Building Expertise for India's Space Future

Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST)

IIST is a premier institution that offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in space science and technology. It serves as a vital source of skilled manpower for ISRO.

Key Features:

  • Asia's first Space University.
  • Curriculum tailored to the needs of the Indian space program.
  • Strong emphasis on research and development.

ISRO Training Centre (ITRAC)

ITRAC is responsible for the induction training of newly recruited scientists and engineers. The training is designed to provide a comprehensive overview of ISRO's activities and to develop the necessary skills for a successful career in the space sector.

Training Modules:

  • Foundation course in space technology.
  • Specialized modules on launch vehicles, satellites, and applications.
  • Project work and on-the-job training at various ISRO centers.
