Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST)

Programme Details

About IIST

Established in 2007, the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) is a Deemed to be University under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. It is an autonomous institution under the Department of Space, created to provide a dedicated stream of highly skilled manpower for the Indian Space Programme.

Academic Programmes Offered

  • B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering: Focuses on the design and development of aircraft, spacecraft, and launch vehicles.
  • B.Tech in Avionics: Specializes in the electronics systems used in aviation and space technology.
  • Dual Degree (B.Tech + M.S./M.Tech): An integrated 5-year programme with specializations in space-related disciplines.
  • M.Tech and Ph.D. Programmes: Offered in various specializations like RF & Microwave Engineering, Geoinformatics, and Earth System Science.

The ISRO Absorption Scheme

A key feature of IIST is the direct recruitment opportunity for its top students. Students who maintain a CGPA of 7.5 or above (on a 10-point scale) throughout their course are eligible for direct absorption as Scientist/Engineer 'SC' at various ISRO centres, subject to the availability of vacancies.

Admission Process

Admission to B.Tech programmes is based on the ranking in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced. Admission to M.Tech programmes requires a valid GATE score. The application window typically opens in May for undergraduate and March for postgraduate courses.
