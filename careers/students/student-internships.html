Student Internships & Projects

Programme Details

Objective

The ISRO Internship Programme is designed to provide students with a unique opportunity to bridge the gap between their academic knowledge and its practical application in the high-technology domain of space science. Interns work on active ISRO projects under the mentorship of our leading scientists and engineers.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to Indian citizens pursuing B.E./B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, or M.Sc./Ph.D. in relevant disciplines.
  • Candidates must have completed at least four semesters of their undergraduate course or one semester of their postgraduate course.
  • A minimum aggregate of 70% marks or a CGPA of 7.5/10 is required.

Duration & Stipend

Internships are offered for a minimum period of 45 days and a maximum of 6 months. A monthly stipend is provided to cover accommodation and expenses. The exact duration depends on the project's requirements and the student's academic calendar.

Application Process

Applications must be forwarded by the Head of the Institution. The application package should include academic transcripts for all semesters, a No Objection Certificate from the college, and a detailed Statement of Purpose outlining the student's area of interest.

Application Deadlines: March 31st for the summer session and September 30th for the winter session.
