Student Internships & Projects

ISRO offers opportunities for students to undertake internships and academic projects at various centers. This provides invaluable hands-on experience in the space industry.

Key Areas:

  • Rocketry and Launch Vehicle Technology
  • Satellite Design and Development
  • Remote Sensing and GIS Applications
  • Space Science and Astronomy

UNNATI Programme

The UNNATI (UNispace Nanosatellite Assembly & Training by ISRO) programme is a capacity-building initiative that offers training on nanosatellite assembly and testing.

Programme Highlights:

  • International collaboration and knowledge sharing.
  • Hands-on training in satellite technology.
  • Aims to develop skilled manpower in the space sector globally.
